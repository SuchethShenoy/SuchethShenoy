# Hi, I'm Sucheth Shenoy 👋

I am a **PhD student in Robot Learning** at the
[Intelligent Autonomous Systems Lab](https://www.ias.informatik.tu-darmstadt.de/)
at **TU Darmstadt**.

My research lies at the intersection of **Robot Learning, Optimal Transport and
Control Theory**. I am particularly interested in
developing learning-based control methods with stability and robustness
guarantees for real-world robotic systems.

Previously, I completed my **M.Sc. in Mechatronics** at Hamburg University of
Technology (TUHH), specializing in Robotics and Intelligent Systems. I conducted
my master's thesis at the DECODE group at EPFL on reinforcement learning for
optimal control with stability guarantees.

## 🔬 Research Interests

- **Safe and Robust Reinforcement Learning**  
  Learning-based controllers with stability, robustness, and performance
  guarantees.

- **Optimal Transport and Distributionally Robust Optimization**  
  Distributional methods for learning, estimation, and robust control.

- **Optimal Control and Estimation**  
  Control, probabilistic filtering, and decision-making for dynamical systems.

- **Robot Learning**  
  Connecting learning-based methods with optimal transport and control theory for real-world robotic
  systems.

## 🚀 Research Highlights

### Stability-Constrained Reinforcement Learning

**MAD: A Magnitude And Direction Policy Parametrization for
Stability-Constrained Reinforcement Learning**

A policy parameterization that incorporates state-dependent feedback into
reinforcement learning while preserving closed-loop stability for nonlinear
dynamical systems.

### Koopman-Based Identification and Predictive Control

**Temporal Autoencoder for Identification and Predictive Control of Nonlinear
Dynamics based on Koopman Operator Theory**

Research on learning finite-dimensional Koopman representations of nonlinear
dynamical systems using autoencoders. The learned models enable prediction and
control using linear systems and model predictive control methods.

[![Paper](https://img.shields.io/badge/Paper-ACC%202025-00629B?style=flat-square&logo=ieee)](https://doi.org/10.23919/ACC63710.2025.11108016)

## 🤖 Projects

### Petoi Bittle Quadruped Locomotion

Developed a reinforcement learning environment for autonomous gait generation
and step climbing with the Petoi Bittle quadruped robot. The project includes
PyBullet simulation environments and policies trained using PPO, TD3, and DDPG.

[![Repository](https://img.shields.io/badge/Repository-OpenCat%20Gym-181717?style=flat-square&logo=github)](https://github.com/SuchethShenoy/opencat-gym)

### Computer-Vision-Guided Robotic Needle Placement

Developed a perception and robotic-control pipeline for needle placement using
3D point-cloud information, an Azure Kinect camera, ROS, and a Franka Emika
Panda robot.

[![Repository](https://img.shields.io/badge/Repository-CV%20Guided%20Needle%20Placement-181717?style=flat-square&logo=github)](https://github.com/ZeitDev/cv_guided_needle_placement)
